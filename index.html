ABOUT US



Our vision is to revolutionize the way middle-class
 families in India commute by providing them with
 efficient and economical electronic cycles.
 We believe that electric bicycles are the
 future of transportation, and our goal
 is to make them accessible to everyone.

  

OUR VISION



Our goal is to make electric cycles affordable
 and accessible to everyone, especially in
 rural areas. We envision a future where
 electric cycles will replace petrol/diesel
 bikes and will become the primary mode of
 transportation for daily commutes.

At Bharat E+, we are committed to building
 a sustainable future and contributing
 towards reducing air pollution in India. 
We believe that our efforts towards a 
greener future will not only benefit the
 environment but also improve the quality
 of life for millions of people.
